The primary difference between an escrow transaction and an OTC transaction is the addition of an agent or buyer/seller mandates. In the escrow process, Crypto Escrow LLC  acts as a neutral party to settle a trade brought forward by an agent or buyer/seller mandate. In the OTC space, Crypto Escrow LLC acts as the agent to negotiate a trade between two parties and conducts settlement.
